About Me

Hello there! My name is Izan, my pronouns are she/her, and I am an aspiring front-end developer with a love for web development and user interface design. I graduated from Stony Brook University with a B.S. in Information Systems, specializing in System & Network Administration and Technological Systems Management.

I am fascinated with all the different ways data and interfaces are used to create user views. My background in information technology, combined with my interest in programming and design, pushes me to think outside the box and be creative.

Currently, I am a self-taught developer building up my portfolio after immersing myself in learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, ES6, Regular Expressions and React.

When I'm not building projects you can find me drawing, exercising and trying popular food and drinks.
